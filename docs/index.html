üåç What is World Community Grid?

World Community Grid (WCG) is a humanitarian computing grid that brings together volunteers from around the world to create the largest non-profit computing grid benefiting humanity. WCG is making technology available only to public and not-for-profit organizations to use in critical research on problems affecting the health and well-being of our planet.

By donating my computer's idle processing power, I'm helping scientists tackle some of humanity's most pressing challenges in health and sustainability.
