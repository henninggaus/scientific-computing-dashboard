Rosetta@home & Nobel Prize 2024

I've been contributing to Rosetta@home since June 2018, supporting David Baker's research on computational protein design at the University of Washington.

On 9 October 2024, Baker received 50% of the Nobel Prize in Chemistry for this work. The Rosetta software was cited in the Nobel Committee's scientific background.
